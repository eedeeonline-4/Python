# Tmdb

## Project Overview
This analysis provides insights into the most profitable movies, genres, directors, production companies, and yearly trends in the movie industry based on the TMDB dataset.

## Key Insights
Yearly Analysis:
The year with the highest number of movies was 2014 (700 movies), followed by 2013 (659 movies) and 2015 (629 movies). ​
The year with the highest total profit was 2015, with a profit of $19 billion.

Top 10 Movies by Profit:
The top 10 movies with the highest profit include "Avatar," "Star Wars: The Force Awakens," "Titanic," "Jurassic World," and "Furious 7," among others. ​

Directors with Most Movies:
Woody Allen directed the most movies (45), followed by Clint Eastwood (34), Martin Scorsese (29), and Steven Spielberg (29)
​
Genres Analysis:
The genre with the highest average budget was "Adventure|Fantasy|Action|Western|Thriller" ($425 million). 8​
The genre with the highest total profit was "Comedy" ($12.18 billion), followed by "Drama" ($9.05 billion) and "Comedy|Romance" ($7.82 billion).

Top 10 Highest Rated Movies:
The highest-rated movies include "The Story of Film: An Odyssey" (9.2), "The Mask You Live In" (8.9), and "Life Cycles" (8.8). 
​
Top 10 Production Companies:
Paramount Pictures produced the most movies (156) and earned the highest total revenue ($8.09 billion), followed by Universal Pictures (133 movies, $3.79 billion revenue) and Warner Bros. (84 movies, $2.54 billion revenue)
